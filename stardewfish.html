Stardew average fish price calculator

This is a calculator to calculate the average price of fish you would get per area, weather, season and time.

There are sliders below to change the chances whether you get a Perfect Catch, or catch it at all, based on difficulty.
(Lava eels are very good income, but they would become incredible if you could catch one perfect every single time.)

This assumes that fishers are Level 10, not because all fishers would be, but because changing the level doesn't change like the best locations to go, it just changes the average price by the same modifier.

This also assumes that you are fishing in the maximum possible distance for the area. It would change the results slightly if you fished close to the shore for every location, but not by that much, and it's not that difficult to fish at the maximum distance, which is 5 tiles away from land.

THIS IS NOT DONE! Currently this makes the assumption that only fish that you reel in are sold, and everything else, like trash or jelly, is not sold. This is poor function as jelly, which is not extremely rare, sells at 200g, the same as the base price of a catfish, so definitely not nothing.
